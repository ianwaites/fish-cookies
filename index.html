About Us.

Pat's Salmon Cookies is a three-generational Salmon cookie recipe that's been handed down from grandfather to son to grandson (whose father name was Pat). For over 30 years we’ve made these cookies for family and friends. We then sold them to a neighborhood grocery store and the response has been so great that we decided to share this ultimate cookie experience with the rest of the world.

Thank you for choosing Pat’s Salmon Cookies. You will always get our very best. Enjoy our consistently fresh taste cookie after cookie. Are you ready?

When you say Pat’s Salmon Cookies, you’ve said a mouthful. We’ve learned that there’s a powerful demand for what we are passionate about, an excellent Salmon cookie. Our mission is to keep it simple by using the highest quality of ingredients. Pats’s cookies contain no hydrogenated oils, zero grams of trans fat, and absolutely no artificial preservatives, flavors or colors.
